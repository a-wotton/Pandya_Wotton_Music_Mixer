Help Section

To use the music mixer, click and drag on one of the discs and drag it into the empty slot. This will begin to play a track, and you can fill in more spaces in order to build a song. It is recommended to include at least one of each disc type, being drums, bass and melody. The control bar includes pause and play functions that will work on a click. Play around with the music mixer and have fun!
